Where It Began

From a single loom
to a living heritage

"Every thread carries the memory of the hands that wove it."

Aaraina was founded with a simple yet profound intention — to reconnect modern India with the extraordinary artisans who have shaped its textile identity for centuries. We work directly with weaver families across six states, bypassing intermediaries to ensure fair wages and authentic craft.

Our name, Aaraina, means mirror in several Indian languages. We believe a saree is more than fabric — it reflects culture, story, and the woman who drapes it. Each piece in our collection is a window into a weaving tradition that spans generations.

What We Stand For

Principles behind every thread

— 01

Ethical Sourcing

We work directly with weaver cooperatives and family workshops, ensuring fair wages and transparent supply chains. No middlemen, no compromises.

— 02

Living Tradition

Every saree is made using traditional techniques passed down across generations — pit looms, fly-shuttle, zari inlay. We document and preserve these practices so they endure.

— 03

Conscious Design

We believe sustainable fashion begins with slow fashion. Our collections are intentionally curated — fewer pieces, greater meaning, longer life.

— 04

Authentic Origins

Every weave is verified for its regional authenticity. A Kanjivaram is woven in Kanchipuram. A Maheshwari is made in Maheshwar. No imitations.

— 05

Artisan Stories

Behind every saree is a name, a family, a village. We share these stories because craft without context is just fabric.

— 06

Timeless Over Trend

We don't follow fashion cycles. We curate pieces that are as relevant in 2060 as they are today — wearable heirlooms, not seasonal impulse buys.

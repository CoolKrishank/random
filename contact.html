Common Questions

Before you write to us

Do you ship across India?

Yes, we ship to all major cities and towns across India. International shipping is available on request.

Can I order a custom handloom saree?

Absolutely. Our Atelier Handloom collection is made-to-order. Reach out with your requirements and we'll work with our weavers to create something special.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for ready-to-ship items. Custom handloom orders are non-returnable once weaving has begun.

How do I care for my saree?

Dry clean is recommended for silk and zari sarees. Cotton weaves can be hand-washed in cold water with mild detergent. We include care instructions with every order.
